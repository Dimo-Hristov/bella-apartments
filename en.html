The Apartment

Welcome to our cozy one-bedroom apartment! The apartment offers everything you need for a comfortable stay for up to 4 people. It is located in the village of Raduil, just 9 km from the mountain ski resort Borovets and 9 km from the village of Dolna Banya, known for its mineral pools. Enjoy a wonderful vacation in our modern apartment, located in a gated complex with a beautiful mountain view.

  • The living room is spacious, with a sofa bed that converts into a comfortable bed for two.

  • The kitchenette is fully equipped so you can prepare your favorite dishes.

  • The bedroom is spacious and bright, with a 160/200 bed and an incredible mountain view.

  • The bathroom is stylish, with a jacuzzi and everything you need (shower gel, shampoo, soap, towels)

The Complex

The apartment is located in a gated complex, and in front of it unfolds a spacious meadow with an incredible view, which you can enjoy from the bedroom and walk your pet in the midst of nature.

  • Turn your gatherings with friends into unforgettable moments with the complex's party canopy.

  • The complex has a small heated pool for cooling off during hot summer days.

  • Just 9 km away is one of the most popular winter resorts in Bulgaria - Borovets.

  • The village of Dolna Banya, known for its mineral pools, is also just 9 km away.
